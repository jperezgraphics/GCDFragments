Magazine Colour Trends

Ilona Porter

design@ilona.graphics

ilona.graphics/

Magazine Colour Trends explores the use of colour and the quantities in which it is applied in modern magazine design through visual analysis. It is primarily a comparative research project exploring the differences and similarities between GQ and Glamour magazine.

The installation shows the 5 most prominent colours used on each page in the magazine.

The accompanying booklets show a visual representation of the quantities of colour used throughout the fashion sections.
