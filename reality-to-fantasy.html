stef192k@gmail.com  stef192a.wordpress.com

My interest and passion for visual narratives has led me to design a range of comic book outcomes featuring characters and stories on the theme of reality and fantasy. The reality of our everyday lives is mixed with dreams and imaginings that inform our experience of the world we live in. I have explored different forms and formats that attempt to support and reflect the ideas contained in the storylines.
