The aim of this project is to raise consumer awareness about exploitation and the environmental impacts of manufacture within the fashion textile industry. I have devised a campaign, based around the re-presentation of garment ‘care labels’, designed to include surprising facts and statistics aimed to provoke responsible consumerism. Large-scale care labels would be placed within a window display context, and the range of smaller labels are intended as gorilla-style interventions on actual in-store items. An accompanying website includes social media feeds.
