The first in a planned series of publications featuring street art, SA magazine, is intended as an immersive and informative guide to specific locations. The magazine itself, features images of individual street artists work, and includes interviews and discussions. It serves to document and record the ever-changing artworks that inhabit urban environments. Paired with augmented reality, readers may use their smart phones and tablets to further interact with the artwork in a personalized and immersive way.
