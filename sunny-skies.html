SUNNY

SKIES

Sophia Wilson

sofia.madeleine.w@gmail.com

My major project is a graphic novel featuring a young girl named Sunny who experiences the five stages of grief (denial, anger, bargaining, depression, and acceptance). The range of emotional states contained within the subject of life and death allowed me to explore the tone and graphic language used. The non-linear order of the stages of grief have also been explored. The final outcome is presented in black and white to reflect mood and atmosphere. Alternatively, readers are invited to use it as a colouring book, allowing them to further engage with the work.We need to dream.
