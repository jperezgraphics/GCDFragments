There Is No Place is an exhibition concept exploring the theme of Utopia. It shows how varied our imaginations are – revealing the potential we have to build entire realities inside our minds. The concept allows individuals to interpret the exhibition in their own way, creating many iterations of the same idea. The fabric of our lives is never stationary, so our minds shouldn’t be. We need to dream, and this space encourages contemplation about the world as it is now, and how it may be in the future.
